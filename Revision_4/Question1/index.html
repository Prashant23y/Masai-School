Prabhas Raju
Actor

Prabhas was born on 23rd October 1979 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. His actual name is Venkata Satyanarayana PrabhasRaju Uppalapati. He is an Indian Actor who works mostly in Telugu cinema. He is best known for his films like Varsham, Chatrapathi, Bujjigadu and Baahubali: The Beginning.
